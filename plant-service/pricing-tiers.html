At Plant Pulse, we offer affordable options to help you care for your plants effortlessly. Whether you need the essential IoT device, extra hardware for specialized care, or a comprehensive app experience, our pricing tiers ensure you can choose exactly what suits your plants’ needs—and your budget. Explore our tiers below to discover the right fit for your plant care journey!

IoT Device – Essential Care @ ₹1299

Get started with the basics through our IoT Device, designed for easy attachment to any pot. This device is your plant’s new best friend, equipped to automatically water plants and track key health metrics like moisture levels and sunlight exposure. It’s the ideal foundation for hassle-free plant care, ensuring your plants stay hydrated and monitored, even on your busiest days. With the IoT Device, you’re setting up a reliable, self-sustaining care system that makes plant maintenance effortless.

Additional Hardware – Enhanced Plant Support @ ₹299

Take your plant care to the next level with our Additional Hardware package. Perfect for plant lovers seeking specialized support, this tier includes accessories like a solar-powered light stick for optimizing sunlight exposure and a precision water dispenser for customized hydration. These tools work seamlessly with our IoT device to create a holistic care experience, addressing your plants’ unique needs and promoting healthier, happier growth.

App Subscription – Ultimate Guidance and Community at ₹49/month

Elevate your plant care experience with our App Subscription, unlocking a world of knowledge and connection. With guides tailored to specific plant species, the app offers valuable insights to keep your plants thriving. You’ll also gain access to a community of fellow plant parents, where you can exchange tips, ask questions, and share progress with enthusiasts locally and globally. For only ₹49 a month, enjoy expert advice and a supportive network right at your fingertips, enriching your plant care journey every step of the way.

Live Pictures

Live pictures, or living picture walls, are a brilliant way to add a burst of greenery to a blank office wall – these unique planting displays create a great talking point for client-facing areas. The live picture is a self-contained unit that can be fixed to your office wall much like a regular picture. They come in a range of sizes from 70cm to 1m long.
